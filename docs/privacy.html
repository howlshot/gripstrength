Overview

GripStrength is designed to work offline and does not require an account. This policy explains what information the app does (and does not) collect.

Summary: We do not collect, sell, or share personal data. Bluetooth is used only to connect to your dynamometer.

Information we collect

GripStrength does not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location.

  • No account creation.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs.

Bluetooth

The app uses Bluetooth to connect to a Jamar Smart Hand Dynamometer and display grip-strength readings in real time. Bluetooth access is used only for device discovery, connection, and reading measurement data from the paired device.

The app does not use Bluetooth to track location, and it does not share Bluetooth data with third parties.

Data storage

If the app stores any measurements or settings, they are stored locally on your iPad for app functionality. The app does not upload your measurements to a server unless you add features that explicitly do so.
